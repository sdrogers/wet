The McCrea Financial Services West End Tournament 2015 (15th-29th August)

Welcome to the homepage for the McCrea Financial Services Glasgow West End Tennis Tournament. This year the tournament will be hosted by Western Fitness and Rackets Club, running from the 15th to the 29th of August, with matches being played across the participating clubs. More details here.

Juniors

The closing dates for the junior events at the McCrea West End Tournament is the 6th August 2015. Entries can be made at the following link: http://www3.lta.org.uk/Competitions/Competition/?ctn=1-1LCL753

The U8 (Red), U9 (Orange), U10 (Green) will run over the 22nd/23rd August. The draw will be split over Saturday and Sunday afternoon so you only need to be available for one of the two days.

U12, U14, U16 singles and doubles will be played in the evenings over the week beginning 17th August with some matches being played the following week.

Players in all age groups must be able to play on finals day (29th August) should they reach it.

If you have any queries regarding the junior age group events at the McCrea West End tournament please email westerntenniscoachingclub@gmail.com
