Tournament details

For 2016, the tournament is changing format. It's always been hard to schedule matches, and in 2015 far too many had to be cancelled due to player availability. To help ease the pressure, we've extended the tournament from two to four weeks and, rather than scheduling early rounds in senior events, it will be up to players to play them (at their home club) by a particular date. Later rounds will be scheduled.
