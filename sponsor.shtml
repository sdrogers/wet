Tweener - locate, challenge, play

Tweener is a great new application which allows tennis players of all ability ranges to find, challenge and play more matches. Whether you are a social player or high level tournament player Tweener offers something new and refreshing for your tennis life.

Within the app there are a number of cool features: geolocate clubs/parks/players; a simple in app challenge system; take part in fun events; play for awards; easily build a network of new tennis friends; join in with the UK's largest amateur tennis event.

Tennis is meant to be fun and so is Tweener so download now and grow your tennis network.
